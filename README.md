**Cross Border Compliance Audit Engine**

**Project Overview**

This repository contains a deterministic, rule based auditing matrix designed to evaluate automated systems against transnational labor and artificial intelligence regulations. The platform maps cross border behavioral datasets against hardcoded legal boundaries, providing an unhallucinated compliance scorecard for multinational organizations.

**Strategic Research Imperative**

As global technology frameworks increasingly govern public and private pipelines, this project serves as a practical execution of **"Institutional Hospitality."** By encoding complex, qualitative legal statutes into strict mathematical data filters, the platform evaluates where technological optimization degrades into automated neglect. 

This research agenda targets the computational sociology of algorithmic management, offering a methodology to protect student and worker time sovereignty from algorithmic biases.

**Transnational Statutes Hardcoded**

The script enforces deterministic checks across three primary global regulatory architectures:
**United States (EEOC Guidelines & NYC Local Law 144):** Computes adverse structural selection metrics via the classic **Four Fifths (4/5ths) Rule** ($0.80$ threshold).
**European Union (The EU AI Act):** Audits autonomous workflows for human in the loop protocols and calculates statutory financial liabilities based on global turnover.
**Japan (METI Society 5.0 Guidelines):** Tracks human capital sustainability by computing a custom **Temporal Sustainability Index (TSI)** to flag uncompensated time debt and systemic workforce precarity.
